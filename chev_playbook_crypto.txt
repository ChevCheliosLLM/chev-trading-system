### TRENDING MARKET BEHAVIOUR — CRITICAL
- In a confirmed uptrend (4H making HH/HL, net move > +5%), resistance levels are CONTINUATION ZONES. The market is expected to break them, not reverse from them. Shorting resistance in an uptrend is the single lowest-probability trade in crypto — historical win rate < 15%.
- In a confirmed downtrend (4H making LH/LL, net move < -5%), support levels are CONTINUATION ZONES. The market is expected to break them. Longing support in a downtrend fails ~85% of the time.
- The correct response in a STRONG UPTREND is: wait for a dip to a higher low support → go LONG. Not: short every resistance.
- Counter-trend trades (short in uptrend / long in downtrend) require ALL of: (1) A+ score ≥ 1.5× the normal threshold (crypto = 15), (2) confirmed RSI divergence — not forming, (3) explicit 4H BOS or CHoCH proving reversal. Any one missing → SKIP.
